Welcome to Recipe Exchange

HOW TO USE RECIPE EXCHANGE

Exploring Recipe

Browse Recipes: Simply scroll through our homepage or visit the 'Recipes' page to discover a variety of dishes.
Viewing a Recipe: Click on any recipe title or image to view the full recipe, including ingredients, instructions, and preparation time.

Searching for Recipe

Using the Search Bar: If you're looking for something specific, use our search bar. Just type in a keyword (like "chocolate" or "pasta") and hit enter.
Filtering Recipes: Some pages might offer filters by categories such as 'Vegetarian', 'Quick Meals', or 'Desserts'. Use these to narrow down your choices.

Sharing Your own Recipe

Create an Account: To share your recipes, first create an account with us by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button.
Submitting a Recipe: Once logged in, click on 'Submit a Recipe' and fill in the details like title, ingredients, instructions, and upload a photo of your dish.

Interacting With Community

Commenting: Under each recipe, there's a section where you can leave comments, tips, or ask questions about the recipe.
Rating Recipes: You can also rate recipes you've tried, which helps others in the community make decisions.
